Planning:

1: The Consultation & Booking: We meet via video call to share your vision and secure your wedding date. We’ll discuss your custom love story and ceremony style in English, Spanish, or Bilingual.
​​
2: Legal Paperwork: You must obtain your license at any Florida Clerk of the Court office. Remember: Florida residents have a 3-day waiting period, while non-residents can marry the same day.
​​
3: The Ceremony: On your big day, I arrive 30 minutes early to South Florida’s top venues—from Miami Beach to Palm Beach—to coordinate every detail with your planner and production team for a stress-free experience.
​​
4: Certification & Mailing: After the "I do," we sign and handle the legal marriage filing within 48 hours. Your official marriage certificate will arrive by mail shortly after. We handle the paperwork; you enjoy the moment.
